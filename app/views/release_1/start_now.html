Submit your scheme valuation reports

Use this service to submit valuation information for a Defined Benefit Scheme.

This service is only for schemes with a valuation effective date on or after 22 September 2024

If your scheme valuation effective date is before 22 September 2024, and hte scheme was in deficit on that date, use the Recovery plan service through Exchange.

We use this information you provide us to check that your scheme is appropriate funded and meets your statutory funding requirements.

How to use this service

During the submission you will need to upload:

  • a signed copy of the statement of strategy
  • a copy of the acturual valuation report, which has been signed off by the trustee(s) and scheme actuary

Schemes in deficit at the valutation effective date will also need to upload:

  • a copy of the recovery plan
  • a copy of schedule of contributions

You will need to have all of this information to hand as any information not submitted will be lost.
