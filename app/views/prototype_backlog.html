Prototype backlog

This page is a private record of our prototype designs and it tells the progress of what we’ve designed and why. The updates published below describe the development of new features to address user needs or changes in the legislation, iterations of existing features, or findings from user research.

Prototype scope

Design and test end-to-end journeys for external users to:
• Register an accont with TPR.
• Login to TPR.
• Register a scheme.
• Manage a scheme including submissions.
• Submit scheme valuation data.

Design and test end-to-end journeys for internal users to:
• Create and manage business rules for submissions.
• Create and manage content.
• Assess scheme submissions.
